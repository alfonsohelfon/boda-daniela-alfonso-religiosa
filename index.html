Where is the wedding?

The Banquet and Reception will be held at a remote location inside "El Desierto de Los Leones." Please note that there is none to very limited cellphone service — we recommend getting there via Uber. For the return, Uber won't be available, so we will be offering transport back to the Westin Santa Fe hotel. If needed, please reserve a spot here.

What should I wear?

The dress code for our wedding is formal attire. For women we recommend long or midi dresses; for men, a tux or a suit — whichever is preferred.

For the religious ceremony held in the synagogue, women are required to cover their shoulders. We recommend bringing a shawl, which can also be worn later in the evening in case it gets cooler at night.
